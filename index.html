Bo Kristiansen

Alaigwanani

BK@lnmsupport.dk

Bo Kristiansen was in 2010 nominated as Alaigwanani - Member of the Elder Counsil of a maasai clan in Tanzania.
The clan is called Lyingiri and they are 600 in total.

Bo worked in the period 1982-1998 for 10 years in Kenya and Tanzania for DANIDA (Danish Development Agency)and a NGO called Mellemfolkeligt Samvirke (MS).

Bo has for the last 10 years visited Tanzania minimum once a year.

Bo is the Founder of the NGO Lake Natron Maasai Support.

Bo received the order "Paul Harris Fellow" nominated by Rotary Præstø for the work and support towards the Maasais of Lake Natron, Tanzania.

Bo is also used as Guest speaker: The White Maasai
